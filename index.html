📖 How to Play

🎮 Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD - Move left/right
  • Space or Up Arrow - Jump

🎯 Objectives

  • 🪙 Collect coins for points
  • 👹 Avoid or jump on enemies
  • 🏁 Reach the flag to complete the level
